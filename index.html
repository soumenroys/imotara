Concept (teaser)

It’s not social. It’s not search. It’s not therapy. And it’s definitely not ads.

imotara is a charitable, non-commercial idea. Think of it as a private constellation: dots you choose to connect, with a gentle nudge when a shape emerges. No feeds to chase. No followers to impress. Only context that serves your future self.

Signal, not noise

Moments become markers. Markers become meaning.

A lightweight grammar for feelings and reflections that stays local-first and private by design.

Yours, by design

Data sovereignty with reversible consent.

You hold the keys. Sharing is explicit, time-bounded, and revocable. No dark patterns.

Quietly proactive

When the same star blinks twice, it whispers — not shouts.

Nudges are rare, contextual, and fully under your control.

What it is (longer take)

  • A companion that helps you notice patterns across mood, moments, and meaning.
  • Portable memory with humane defaults and graceful exit ramps.
  • Community-stewarded ethics; if it ever becomes software, it stays non-extractive.

What it is not

  • No public timelines, likes, or follower counts.
  • No advertising, profiling, or behavioral targeting — ever.
  • No lock-in. Your stories are yours, always.

Why charitable?

We are working professionals stewarding an idea in our personal time. imotara exists for human benefit and future generations. If it grows, governance will be community-first, and funding will remain donation-supported, not ad-funded.
